How it works

Four simple steps from opportunity to client-ready proposal or pitch deck.

  1. Bring your past wins
    Share past proposals, decks, or project context. Clyff learns what has worked for you — not what works “in general.”
  2. Talk through the opportunity
    Share a few key details: the client, scope, timing, and your point of view. This can happen quickly, conversationally, and without long forms.
  3. See your thinking take shape
    Clyff applies structure and judgment to your input — assembling a clear, on-brand draft that reflects how you actually work. Not generic. Not templated. Yours.
  4. Refine, decide, and send
    You review. You adjust. You decide what goes out. Clyff supports the process — but you’re always in control.
